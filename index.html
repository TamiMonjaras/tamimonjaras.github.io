(A.K.A Cartoons/Anime/Disney movies... you know what I mean)
Any story told by an amount of images moving made by some people is considered an animated film, series, show, whatever. However, I'm going to focus on what is usually considered a "cartoon"... actually, I'm not sure what is the definition, I'm not an expert, I just like watching them.
Why I like it? Oh, right, let's get to the point: the reason why I love watching animated series is because some of them have a great plot, story, characters, design, soundtrack,etc; but, what differences them from a movie, play and other visual forms of entertainement is that I love the drawing and colouring made by the animators, the style of the expressions and actions, the effort and work behind every frame. Most of cartoons are colorful and cheerful. Some, are sort of darker. There are some examples:

Steven Universe is an American animated series for Cartoon Network, created by Rebecca Sugar, who has previously worked on Adventure Time. This series tells the story of a 14 year old boy named Steven Universe, who lives in the fictional town of Beach City with the "Crystal Gems" – Pearl, Garnet, and Amethyst, three magical humanoid aliens. Steven, who is half-Gem (from his mother), goes on adventures with his friends and helps the Crystal Gems protect the world from their own kind.

It has:
  • Great songs and soundtrack
  • Deep backstory and plot
  • Diverse cast design
  • LGBT representation
  • Heartwarming life lessons and a message of love

Gravity Falls is an American animated series produced by Disney Television Animation. Created by Alex Hirsch, the series follows the adventures of the Pine Twins, Dipper and Mabel, who are sent to spend the summer with their great-uncle "Grunkle" Stan, in a mysterious town full of paranormal forces and supernatural creatures. The kids help Stan run "The Mystery Shack", the tourist trap that he owns, while also investigating the local mysteries.

 It has:
  • Good jokes, some dark humor
  • Pretty backgrounds
  • Entertaining plot, smartly written
  • Some backstory, misteries to solve (even symbols and codes hidden)
  • Characters that will win your heart
  • ILLUMINATI

Star vs. the Forces of Evil is an American animated series produced by Disney Television Animation, created by Daron Nefcy. Nefcy is the second woman to create an animated series for this company. The plot revolves around Star Butterfly, a princess from a dimension called Mewni, who gets a magical wand and is sent for "security measures" to Earth. There, she meets Marco, and both of them lives magical adventures.

It has:
  • Fun episodes to watch
  • Magic stuff
  • Likeable characters (there's a hot demon so)
  • (Yeah, backstories)
  • THE POWER OF FRIENDSHIP
